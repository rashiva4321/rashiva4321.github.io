Tape Measure Yagi - June 13, 2026

Did you know tape measure actually has metal in it? yeah. Metal is great for making radio antenna elements. I used 4 "elements" of tape measure, a boom made out of PVC, and zip ties and duct tape to hold it together. I also used RG 174 coax (PICTURE) which I beautifully stripped and used as the feedline for a dipole. If you don't know what a dipole is, its a type of antenna that consists of elements arranged across from each other seperated electrically. A yagi is a dipole but with one long element behind it called the reflector, which reflects the dipole signal and the director in front of the dipole which focuses the signal. This focusing causes a yagi to be directional. (PICTURE)
